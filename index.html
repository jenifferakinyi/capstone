About Us

Built for the farmer
who was left behind

FarmUP was created because rural poultry farmers — especially women and youth — face a system that wasn't designed for them. Veterinary help is expensive and far away. Loan applications get rejected. Markets are unpredictable.

We built an AI ecosystem that works on low-bandwidth connections, speaks to farmers in their context, and turns their daily farm data into the financial records that unlock credit.

Our platform supports layer chick and kienyeji chicken farmers from the moment birds arrive to the moment they reach the market.
